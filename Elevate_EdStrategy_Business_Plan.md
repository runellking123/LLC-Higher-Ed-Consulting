# 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

## 1.1 Company Overview

**Elevate EdStrategy Group, LLC** is a higher education consulting firm founded by four accomplished professionals with combined expertise spanning institutional research, data analytics, student success, strategic planning, accreditation, and organizational leadership. Headquartered with operations across Louisiana, Texas, and North Carolina, the company is positioned to serve colleges, universities, and educational organizations nationwide.

## 1.2 Mission Statement

To empower higher education institutions with data-driven strategies, innovative solutions, and expert guidance that enhance institutional effectiveness, improve student outcomes, and drive sustainable success.

## 1.3 Vision Statement

To become the premier consulting partner for institutions seeking transformative change through evidence-based decision-making and strategic excellence in higher education.

## 1.4 Core Values

- **Excellence:** Delivering the highest quality consulting services
- **Integrity:** Maintaining ethical standards in all engagements
- **Innovation:** Applying cutting-edge methodologies and technologies
- **Collaboration:** Partnering authentically with institutional stakeholders
- **Impact:** Focusing on measurable, meaningful outcomes

# 3. MARKET ANALYSIS

## 3.1 Industry Overview

### Higher Education Consulting Market

The higher education consulting market in the United States represents a significant and growing sector:

- **Market Size (2025):** Approximately $2.8 billion
- **Projected Growth Rate:** 6-8% annually through 2030
- **Number of U.S. Institutions:** ~3,900 degree-granting institutions
- **Total Higher Education Employment:** ~3.9 million

### Key Market Drivers

1. **Enrollment Challenges:** Declining demographics and increased competition for students
2. **Accreditation Pressures:** Heightened scrutiny from accrediting bodies and the Department of Education
3. **Financial Sustainability:** Rising costs and declining state appropriations
4. **Technology Transformation:** Need for data analytics and digital capabilities
5. **Student Success Focus:** Increased emphasis on retention, completion, and outcomes
6. **Regulatory Compliance:** Complex federal and state reporting requirements
7. **DEI Initiatives:** Institutional commitment to diversity, equity, and inclusion

| Technology failure | Low | Low | Low | Cloud-based systems, backups |

## 10.2 Detailed Risk Mitigation

### Partner Departure Risk
**Mitigation:**
- Operating agreement includes non-compete and non-solicitation clauses
- 12-month notice period for planned departures
- Buy-sell provisions for ownership transition
- Cross-training to avoid single points of failure
- Client relationships owned by company, not individuals

### Client Concentration Risk
**Mitigation:**
- Policy: No single client exceeds 25% of annual revenue
- Active diversification of client base
- Develop multiple service lines
- Build retainer relationships for recurring revenue
- Geographic diversification

### Economic Downturn Risk
**Mitigation:**
- Maintain 3-month operating reserve
- Variable cost structure (partner draws adjust with revenue)
- Focus on compliance/accreditation (less discretionary spending)
- Offer payment plans for budget-constrained clients
- Develop lower-cost service options

### Project Delivery Risk
**Mitigation:**
- Clear scope documentation in all contracts
- Milestone-based delivery with client sign-offs
- Internal quality review before delivery
- Change order process for scope changes
- Professional liability insurance
