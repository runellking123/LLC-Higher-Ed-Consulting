- Team introductions
- Roles and responsibilities
- Communication protocols
- Initial timeline review

- Progress since last meeting
- Action item review
- Issues and risks
- Upcoming activities
- Resource needs

## Working Session
- Collaborative work on specific deliverables
- Problem-solving sessions
- Data review and analysis
- Document review and feedback

## Review Meeting
- Milestone or deliverable review
- Quality assessment
- Stakeholder feedback
- Approval decisions

## Closeout Meeting
- Final deliverable review
- Lessons learned
- Knowledge transfer
- Next steps and recommendations
